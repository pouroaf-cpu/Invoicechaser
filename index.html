FAQ

Do I need Xero?

No. We can work from Xero reports, MYOB, Excel or PDFs — whatever you’re using.

Why phone calls matter?

Phone outreach recovers significantly more overdue payments than mail alone, because it forces real, two-way engagement.:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Is this automated software?

No. This is a human-run follow-up service combining phone & email outreach — humans get responses that automation alone often misses.

What if a customer disputes?

We stop immediately and escalate to you to handle next steps. We don’t handle legal disputes or debt collection.
