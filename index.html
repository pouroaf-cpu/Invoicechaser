FAQ

Common questions

Do I need Xero?

No Xero? No problem. If you have invoices in PDF/Excel, we can work from that. If you use Xero, we can use overdue reports and add notes against invoices.

Will customers get annoyed?

Not if it’s done properly. We use a polite, professional tone and a consistent schedule. Most late payments happen because people forgot — reminders fix that.

What if an invoice is disputed?

We stop immediately and escalate to you. You decide the next step. We do not handle disputes or debt collection.

How do you bill me?

Monthly invoice from our system. You get a simple breakdown: standard follow-ups, overdue follow-ups, and how the minimum was applied (if relevant).
