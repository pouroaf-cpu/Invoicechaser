Process

How it works

1

Access + rules

Client invites you to their Xero org (invoice view + notes). You agree the follow-up cadence and tone.

2

Overdue list

We pull overdue invoices via Xero (Aged Receivables / Awaiting Payment) and build the action list.

3

Follow-ups

We call/email. Every action is logged in Xero “History & Notes” against the invoice.

4

Monthly invoice

You receive one monthly invoice from us (per follow-up + minimum). Disputes get escalated back to you.
